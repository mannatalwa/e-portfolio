About Us

We understand the importance priortizing our well-being from a well-rounded perspective, focusing on both physical health and mental health, without priortizing only one. This platform was created to provide tools and resources to track your moods and habits, set and achieve goals, and also to connect with a community of like-minded individuals

Mission

At SelfSync, our mission is to empower teens with a personalized digital wellness tracker that makes self-care simple, fun, and effective. We believe that small, mindful habits can lead to a healthier body and mind. Through intuitive tracking and thoughtful reminders, we help you build routines that support your well-being—one step at a time

Goals

  • To encourage healthy habits – Help our users build sustainable routines for physical and mental well-being.
  • To promote self-awareness – Provide insights into mood patterns and wellness trends for better self-care.
  • To make tracking simple and fun – Offer an intuitive, interactive platform that motivates users to stay consistent.

Key Features

  • Easy and Customizable Habit Tracking
  • Efficient Mood Tracking with Daily Logs and Mood Journal
  • View your progress with Monthly Charts and Pixels

Target Audience

Our primary audience is teenagers who are balancing academics, extracurriculars, and personal wellness, seeking a simple and engaging way to build healthy habits.
