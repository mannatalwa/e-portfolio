How to Use

Besides is a cute calendar to help you track your step count! You can adjust your step goal from 7000-12000 using the drop-down menu, and click on the little buttons which represent the dates of a month! Try to stick to the habit till the end of the month for a little surprise! No cheating allowed!

  • Use the drop-down to set your step count goal.
  • Click on a date to mark it as completed.
  • Track your progress with the completed days counter.
  • Use "Previous Month" and "Next Month" buttons to navigate.
  • Press "Reset" to clear all data.
